Gravity & Air Resistance

  • Gravity is a force that causes objects to accelerate towards the ground. The farther up an object is when it is dropped, the faster it is when it hits the ground.
  • Air resistance is when an object is moving towards the ground, while gravity pushes it down, the air below it will push it up.

Momentum

  • P = mv is the formula to calculate momentum of an object. P is the momentum, m is the mass (weight) of the object, and v is the velocity (speed) of the object.
  • The more momentum the egg has when it hits the ground, the more likely it is to break.
  • Try to either reduce the mass or the velocity of the egg to lower the chances of it breaking.
