Catapult

Tension: Rubber Band

  • When you stretch a rubber band, you create Tension.
  • When you let go of it, it un-stretches back into its original size, and the energy is released, which makes it fly

Catapult: Physics

  • A catapult is a device that is used to “throw” heavy objects long distances using tension.
  • Most have a bucket, an arm, a crossbar, a payload, and a spring.
  • The spring is tensioned, and when the tension is released, energy is transferred to the arm, which throws the payload.
